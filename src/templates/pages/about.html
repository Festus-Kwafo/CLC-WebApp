Mission & Ministry

Our Mission

Christ’s Abounding Love Church is a Global Evangelical ministry with its headquarters in Accra, Ghana.
We are passionate about reaching others with the Gospel. We are a local church with a global mission of reaching the ends of the world for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Ministry

The work to reach the lost with the Gospel has already begun through several Gospel crusades.
The Life & Purpose Conference and Help From Above Conference are annually held to incite many to fulfill the purpose of God for their lives.
